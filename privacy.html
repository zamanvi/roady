3. How We Share Your Information

Roady does not sell your personal information to third parties.

3.1 With Other Platform Users (Limited)

  • When a Provider wins a bid or accepts an Urgent job, their first name, rating, ETA, and a Twilio proxy phone number (not their real number) are shared with the Customer.
  • When a job is accepted, the Customer's Twilio tracking number (not their real number) is shared with the Provider for coordination.
  • Real phone numbers are never directly shared between customers and providers through the Platform.

3.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, user information may be transferred as a business asset. We will notify you via in-app notice or email before your data is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

4. Location Data

Location data is the most sensitive information we handle. Here is exactly how we use it:

  • Customer location: Collected when you share it for a service request. Used only to match you with nearby providers and for the active job. Not stored permanently after job completion (only job address is stored for your order history).
  • Provider location: Collected in real-time during active jobs for navigation and customer tracking. Not stored long-term. Used in aggregate (without identifying individual providers) to improve dispatch algorithms.
  • We never track your location in the background when you are not actively using the Platform for an active job.

  • Correction: Update inaccurate information via account settings or by contacting us.
  • Deletion: Delete your account via the app. Some data is retained as required by law (see Section 5).
  • Opt-out of marketing: Reply STOP to any SMS, or unsubscribe from email communications at any time.

6.2 EU / EEA / UK Users (GDPR)

  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")

  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Right to lodge a complaint with your local Data Protection Authority (DPA)

Our EU representative contact: privacy@roady.app. Response within 30 days.

6.3 California Residents (CCPA / CPRA)

  • Right to delete personal information.
  • Right to opt-out of sale or sharing (Roady does not sell or share personal information for advertising).

  • Right to limit use of sensitive personal information.

Submit California privacy requests to privacy@roady.app or call our toll-free number once available.

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • TLS 1.2+ encryption for all data in transit.
  • AES-256 encryption for sensitive data at rest.
  • PCI-DSS compliant payment processing via Stripe (we never touch raw card data).
  • Access controls limiting employee access to personal data on a need-to-know basis.
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing.

No system is 100% secure. In the event of a data breach affecting your information, we will notify you as required by applicable law (within 72 hours for GDPR, per state law for US residents).

8. Children's Privacy

The Platform is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we learn we have collected information from a minor, we will delete it immediately. Contact privacy@roady.app if you believe a minor has submitted data.

9. Cookies & Local Storage

The Platform uses browser localStorage and sessionStorage — not third-party advertising cookies. Data stored locally includes:

  • Admin commission configuration (roady_admin_aff)
  • Referral attribution (roady_ref)
  • User session preferences

You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser's site data. No tracking pixels or third-party analytics are currently deployed.

10. Third-Party Links

The Platform may contain links to third-party services (Google Maps, WhatsApp, etc.). This Privacy Policy does not apply to those services. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you access.
